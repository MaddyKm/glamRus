What We Do

GlamR'us offers makeup instruction from professional makeup artists for women who want to learn their own glow!

Whether it's for a big event, your next girls night out, or just to feel more confident in your everyday look. Our small group classes (4-10) make it personal and hands-on - no crowds, no pressure, just a fun experience guided by a professional makeup artist.

Open Classes

Jist of the Open Class

  • 4 hours of instruction from a professional makeup artist
  • Small class size capped at 10 to provide 1:1 feedback and guidance
  • Learn about both skincare and makeup technique
  • Analysis of current products/ tools you own
  • Trial products/ tools you may be missing or want to upgrade
